About BrightDemon SMP

BrightDemon SMP is a community-focused Survival Minecraft server designed for players who enjoy a balanced, fair, and long-term survival experience. We focus on stability, respectful gameplay, and carefully planned updates.

Mods are currently in development and will be introduced gradually to enhance gameplay without breaking survival balance.
